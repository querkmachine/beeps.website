Standard body text is well sized for readability and suitable for being bold, italics, and generally being messed around with.

Lead (or large) body text is used for... well, leading. Think like the big introductory text you see at the start of some sections or pages. That.

Supplementary (or small) body text is for text that is generally less important than the text it's next to.
