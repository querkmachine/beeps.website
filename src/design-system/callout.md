{{ kimExample("callout", "Callout") }}

## Usage

Use when:

- Highlighting important information separate from surrounding content.

Don't use when:

- Providing information tangential to surrounding content, such as an opinion or anecdote. A [character]({{ '/design-system/character/' | url }}) is better suited to that.
