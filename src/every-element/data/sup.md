---
tag: sup
name: Superscript
category: Text semantics
spec: https://html.spec.whatwg.org/multipage/text-level-semantics.html#the-sub-and-sup-elements
---

Inline text that needs to be displayed in superscript (above the text baseline) for typographical reasons, such as mathematical formulae.

<!-- prettier-ignore-start -->
```html
<p>Einstein's theory of relativity stated that E=MC<sup>2</sup>.</p>
```
<!-- prettier-ignore-end -->

Companion to [`sub`](#sub), the subscript element.
